# OCP Java SE 17 Study Notes

Welcome. These are my **personal notes** while preparing for the
**Oracle Certified Professional: Java SE 17 Developer** certification
(Exam **1Z0-829**), written alongside the Sybex *Study Guide* by
Jeanne Boyarsky and Scott Selikoff.

They focus on the tricky, exam-tested details that didn't stick on a first
read, not a full re-teaching of the language.

> **Feel free to use these however you please.** They're shared as-is, with no
> guarantees of completeness or correctness. If they help you on your own OCP
> journey, wonderful.
